# Stakeholder Analysis – PhishGuard

## Stakeholder Overview
Stakeholders are individuals or groups who influence, are impacted by, or have an interest in the success of the PhishGuard project.

Since PhishGuard is a cybersecurity-focused capstone concept, stakeholders include both project participants and potential end users.

---

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. PhishGuard Team
**Role:** Project development team

**Interest:**
- Successful completion of Capstone 1
- Effective Agile/Scrum collaboration
- Strong documentation and deliverables
- Capstone 2 project readiness

**Impact Level:** High
